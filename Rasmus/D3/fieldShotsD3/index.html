Event type

Drag the slider to view the different periods of the game. The 1 represents the events during the first 10 minutes
(0 to 10), the 2 represents the events during the next 10 minutes (10 to 20) and so on. The events are not summed,
such that one can easily compare where events take place during what parts of a game. The events from an entire
season has been aggregated into one view. Select what kind of event you want to see by using the selector above.
